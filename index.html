Code Camp X

Code Camp X is the best management tool for you to use if you have issues managing your coding bootcamp! This will help you save countless of hours by making it simple for you to track the profitability of each of your courses, track the progress of your students and even effectively manage the teachers that you’ve hired! Why wait? Try our application now!

Who is it for?

  • Those who find it difficult to managing a coding bootcamp

  • Those who have issues tracking manpower efficiently

  • Those who care about their students and their progress

  • Those who want to effectively tell the profitability of their bootcamp

  • Those who want to immediately see all the students that they have

Features

  • Student Progress Tracker

  • Student Fee Payment Tracker

  • Manpower Management

  • Financial Analysis of Courses
